Interests

Ever since my childhood, the magic of bringing characters to life fascinated me. In high school, I partook in the animation pathway, and as I progressed, I realized that animation wasn’t just about bringing cartoons to life but so much more. The intricate weaving of several aspects - technical, design, and beauty into one animation and the ability it held to impact people from diverse cultures engrossed me. Yet, its ability to efficiently carry out messages that mere language and writing cannot, captured me the most.

Using animation, I would love to tell stories and communicate emotions in a unique way to people all over the world.
